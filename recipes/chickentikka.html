Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

This is my grandmother's favorite slow cooker Chicken Tikka Masala recipe. I've loved it ever since I was a young child. Indian food is so good, it's easy to see why the spice trade was such a huge deal.

In this recipe, we will get the chicken and spices and other ingredients and put them in the slow cooker of your choice. After several hours, you will have a delicious meal ready to eat! Just cook some basmati rice to go with it!

Fun Fact: Basmati rice is a special kind of aromatic rice grown in Pakistan and India; the name means "full of aroma," which is appropriate given how fragrant it is!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tikka
  • Masala

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken in the Tikka.
  2. Springkle the Masala.
  3. Shut the lid.
