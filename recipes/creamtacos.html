Cream Tacos

Description

Another crock for the pot, this recipe is so easy and simple it'll have your blood pressure rising and your blood sugar spiking in just a few minutes!

All you need to do is buy a bunch of processed, canned food and throw it all in a big crockpot. It's definitely not healthy but BOY is it so delicious and cheesy! It only takes a few minutes to heat up and be ready to ladle over your favorite corn chips.

My aunt allegedly got this recipe somewhere, but I've never heard of anyone else knowing about it. All I can find is people making something called "taco soup" which is not what this is.

Ingredients

  • Browned Beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Chilli
  • Fritos (or tostitos)
  • Shredded cheese
  • Nacho Cheese Soup

Steps

  1. Brown the burger.
  2. Add the taco seasoning.
  3. Empty all the cans into the pot on top of the taco meat.
  4. Let it reach hot enough temperature to melt the cheese you're going to sprinkle on top.
